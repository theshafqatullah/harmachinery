================================================================================

RTA (Roads and Transport Authority) continuously expands Dubai's road network.
These projects run 24/7 and need generators for night work, traffic
management, and construction equipment power.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

   - Bridge construction and flyover projects

     rollers, dump trucks, light towers

     cranes, compactors, concrete equipment, welding generators

================================================================================
  HOW TO WIN GOVERNMENT & INFRASTRUCTURE CONTRACTS
================================================================================

  1. REGISTER AS A SUPPLIER:
     - Dubai Municipality vendor registration
     - RTA supplier database
     - DEWA approved contractor/supplier list
     - Etihad Rail procurement platform
     - Dubai South investment/supplier portal

  2. TARGET THE SUBCONTRACTORS:
     - Main contractors often subcontract equipment rental
     - Build relationships with procurement managers at:
       * ALEC Engineering (major Dubai contractor)
       * Dutco Construction
       * China State Construction
       * Samsung C&T (metro projects)
       * Acciona (infrastructure projects)
       * Gamuda (tunnel projects)
       * Besix (mega projects)

  3. OFFER WHAT GOVERNMENT NEEDS:
     - Compliance certificates for all equipment
     - Regular maintenance schedules
     - Emergency replacement guarantee (24-hour swap)
     - Proper insurance documentation
     - HSE (Health, Safety, Environment) compliance
     - Equipment operator provision (if possible)

  4. PRICING STRATEGY:
     - Government tenders are price-competitive
     - Offer monthly/quarterly rates for long-term contracts
     - Include fuel and maintenance in package deals
     - Offer loyalty discounts for repeat bookings
