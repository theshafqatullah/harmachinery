================================================================================
  YOUR LOCATION ADVANTAGE — AL LISAILI
================================================================================

Al Lisaili sits on the Dubai-Al Ain Road (E66), one of Dubai's major highways.
This gives you a STRATEGIC ADVANTAGE:

  ✓ Direct highway access to southern Dubai's mega developments
  ✓ Close proximity to desert areas (events, camps, safari)
  ✓ Surrounded by hundreds of farm houses (constant event demand)
  ✓ Lower rent/overhead compared to Al Quoz or Ras Al Khor industrial areas
  ✓ Quick access to Dubai South, Expo City, airport expansion projects
  ✓ Emirates Road (E611) accessible for northern Dubai & Sharjah deliveries
  ✓ Less traffic congestion for machinery transport than city-center suppliers
  ✓ Central position between Dubai city and Al Ain (can serve both markets)

================================================================================
  ADDITIONAL SPECIALTY MARKETS
================================================================================

A. FILM & MEDIA PRODUCTION
───────────────────────────
   - Dubai Studio City productions filming in desert/outdoor locations
   - Need silent generators for on-location shoots
   - Generator need: 30–100 kVA (silent models essential)
   - Contact: Production companies, location managers

B. SPORTS EVENTS
─────────────────
   - Marathon and cycling events along Al Ain Road
   - Cricket tournaments in open grounds (DSO, Sports City)
   - Football tournaments on rented pitches
   - Generator need: 20–80 kVA (timing systems, PA, lighting)

C. RELIGIOUS & COMMUNITY EVENTS
────────────────────────────────
   - Ramadan iftar tents (pop-up structures during holy month)
   - Eid prayer grounds (outdoor, need sound system power)
   - Friday markets and community bazaars
   - Generator need: 15–60 kVA

D. REAL ESTATE SHOW HOMES / SALES CENTERS
──────────────────────────────────────────
   - Developers setting up temporary sales offices at new project sites
   - Show villas/apartments need power before grid connection
   - The Valley, Damac Hills 2, Villanova all have/had these
   - Generator need: 50–100 kVA (AC, lighting, AV equipment)
   - RECURRING: Each new project phase = new sales center

E. TELECOM TOWER INSTALLATIONS
───────────────────────────────
   - 5G tower installations across Dubai require temp power
   - Telecom companies (du, Etisalat) and their contractors
   - Generator need: 20–50 kVA
   - DURATION: 1–4 weeks per site

F. ROAD & UTILITY MAINTENANCE
──────────────────────────────
   - RTA road maintenance projects (lighting, signage work)
   - DEWA electrical maintenance operations
   - Water authority pipeline work
   - Generator need: 20–80 kVA
   - FREQUENCY: Ongoing year-round
